About The Builder
FORGE is built to make consistency feel sharp, fast, and a little addictive. It combines a static single-file frontend, Supabase auth/data, and a server-side Gemini layer so the app stays lightweight while the smart features stay private.
Why It Exists
The idea behind FORGE is simple: fewer excuses, cleaner tracking, better momentum. Calories, workouts, AI support, streaks, and social competition all live in one focused system designed by Aswin Menon.
